Adopters.cc: Ecosystem for dapps establishing right participation intent of adopters over Adopters.cc infrastructure


Abstract


Economic interactions are more efficient when need for a subjective trust is eliminated from the system while right intent to participate is enforced. Blockchain provides an immutable ledger that ensures a complete and transparent history, however, it is infeasible for anyone to audit this in real time, therefore a heuristic must be used. This whitepaper aims to guide development of a trust enforcing infrastructure based on users specific interactions with various public ledgers that can be used to summarize their past behaviour and therefore ensure trust in economic transactions. With the proposed infrastructure, proof of loyalty, stakes, intent and expertise will be established and used as per the use case and economics of the system. Such an infrastructure needs governance as well as support for building an ecosystem. Adopters.cc aims to establish this infrastructure and ecosystem of applications and adopters around that.

Crypto-economics way as defined by Vitalik Buterin ( founder of Ethereum ) as a new kind of economics where good participation is incentivised and bad participation is penalised. Right intent of participation can be developed by creating algorithms which incentivises adopters with good intent or action and penalises adopters with bad intention or actions. Adopters.cc provides an infrastructure to build apps leveraging crypt-economics way of doing things. This will help establish marketplace applications where subjective trust was involved, alikes of fiverr, mTurk, elance etc. This paper illustrates example of one such application which will be launched around ICO as well as explain governance utilities for managing the ecosystem which Adopters.cc is working on.

We also introduce Adopters Contribution Coins which enables community contribution and to establish proof of stakes and right intent for the ecosystem.

Introduction


Decentralisation of efforts done in way to maximise benefit of participants when they help drive organisation in direction where organisation wants to move and penalise them when they drive organisation in harmful direction has become one of the biggest economic change to build performant organisations. Uber, AirBnb, Amazon etc are some great examples of fastest growing economies built around this economic theory. With the advent of blockchain, this is much easily possible provided right set of infrastructure. Adopters.cc aims to provide ecosystem, platform, products, infrastructure and tokens required to build fast growing organisations leveraging participation of adopters in a way to maximise participating organisations’ benefits. A lot of research has already been done in establishing long-term orientation for stockholders to establish right intent while participating in work, decisions and voting.

Crypto-economic way is to achieve that same using ledger, loyalty and/or stakes and decentralised way is to achieve that using participation based on expertise. Adopters.cc combines the best of the world economic ways to increase adopters participation in a way where organisations move in directions most beneficial to the ecosystem.

Most used definition of integrity is doing the right thing when nobody is watching and recording such transactions over ledger has created a new economic way of by-passing subjective ways to trust participation. The challenge lies in establishing approaches objectively on a massive scale and there is a need for algorithms capturing such data over ledgers as well leveraging them to create fair participation ecosystem. The cost of finding right intent and integrity of expertise providers is very high and it could be made possible to find expertise providers with right intent using enough proofs available with the advent of crypto economics. In the current economic ways, participants can reveal work they want to reveal and hide ones they consider might establish wrong intent. The integrity of individuals are always considered at minimum and this lack of trust has resulted in a very high cost of engagement. Startups are high performant till the initial days and very minimal companies could grow fast with the same high performance since it is impossible to establish right intent for participation. Expertise of Adopters could never be much leveraged in most of the organisations still.
